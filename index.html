I was born and grew up in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. In 2014, I graduated from Prince of Wales Mini School. As long as I can remember, I have enjoyed building and understanding systems of all kinds.

Currently, I am studying Mathematical Physics and Computer Science at the University of Waterloo. I hope to finish my undergraduate degree in 2018.

I like to spend my free time exploring a wide range of different activities. These are the ones that interest me the most:

Waterloo Baja Team

I'm a member of the University of Waterloo's Baja SAE team. The team builds and races a car every year, competing against other universities from Canada and the United States.

For more information about Baja racing, you can take a look at our team's website, or check out the official Baja SAE competition site.
